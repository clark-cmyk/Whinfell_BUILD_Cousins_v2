## 1. Goal

To provide high-quality, well-documented supporting assets that enable the desk to effectively use the Whinfell Transmission Map — including clear calculation logic, reliable fallback tools, tested prompts, and clean reference materials — while ensuring zero disruption to the live build happening inside Comet.

## 3. Skillify (Required Skills & Tools)

- Strong understanding of the Whinfell Transmission Map framework and Credit Confirmation Score logic
- Proficiency in Excel / Google Sheets for fallback tools
- Ability to write clear, structured documentation
- Familiarity with the 6 refined Agentic Prompts
- Disciplined version control (Git) and review process adherence

---

## 4. Arena Review Process (Mandatory for All Deliverables)

Every major deliverable must go through this sequence:

1. **Self Review** — Owner reviews their own work
2. **Peer Review** — Another Cousin reviews
3. **Arena Review** — Relevant Arena member(s) review
4. **Final Sign-off** — TempLibby approves before release

No deliverable is considered complete until it passes all four gates.

## 6. Success Criteria (How We Know We're Done Well)

- All Priority 1 deliverables pass Arena Review on first or second submission.
- The Credit Confirmation Score logic is clear enough that a trader can calculate it manually in under 2 minutes.
- Fallback tools are genuinely usable when Comet/Koyfin is slow or unavailable.
- All work is properly version controlled and documented.
- The main Comet build continues without being slowed down by the Cousins' work.

## 7. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk                                      | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|-------------------------------------------|------------|--------|----------|
| Cousins over-scope into live Comet work   | Medium     | High   | Strict scope boundaries + weekly check-ins with TempLibby |
